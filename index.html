    • Recipe #1: Foul Medames (Fava Bean Stew)

    Time: 25 minutes
    Serves: 2

  • Ingredients & Quantity

    • Canned fava beans - 1 can (400g)
    Garlic (crushed) - 2 cloves
    Sesam oil - 2 tbsp
    Lemon juice 1 tbsp
    Cumin - 1 tsp
    Salt - To taste
    Fresh parsley - Handful (chopped)
    Chopped tomatoes (optional) - 2 tbsp
    Boiled egg - 1 (optional)

  • Step 1: Drain and rinse the fava beans.
  • Step 2: Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  • Step 3: Add beans, cumin, salt, and tomatoes. Mash slightly.
  • Step 4: Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Step 5: Add lemon juice and sprinkle parsley before serving. Top with boiled egg if desired.
    • Source: Various Sudanese & Middle Eastern recipe websites.

    Recipe #2: Basbousa (Semolina Cake)

    Time: 45 minutes
    Serves: 8

  • Ingredients & Quantity

    • Semolina 1 cup
    Sugar 1 cup
    Yogurt 1 cup
    Desiccated coconut ½ cup
    Baking powder 1 tsp
    Butter (melted) ½ cup
    Almonds (for topping) 8
    Sugar (for syrup) 1 cup
    Water (for syrup) ½ cup
    Lemon juice 1 tsp

  • Step 1: Mix semolina, sugar, coconut, yogurt, butter, and baking powder in a bowl.
  • Step 2: Pour into a greased baking tray and smooth the top.
  • Step 3: Score into squares, top each with an almond.
  • Step 4: Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes until golden.
  • Step 5: While baking, prepare syrup by boiling sugar, water, and lemon juice for 10 minutes.
  • Step 6: Pour cooled syrup over hot basbousa. Let it soak and cool before serving.
